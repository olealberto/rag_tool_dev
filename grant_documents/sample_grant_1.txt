
        NIH GRANT PROPOSAL
        Grant ID: R01MD123456
        Title: Community Health Worker Intervention for Diabetes Prevention
        
        SPECIFIC AIMS
        Aim 1: Implement diabetes prevention program in 5 urban FQHCs
        Aim 2: Train 20 community health workers
        Aim 3: Evaluate cost-effectiveness
        
        BACKGROUND
        Diabetes rates are 50% higher in Latino populations. FQHCs serve as 
        critical access points but lack prevention resources.
        
        METHODS
        Randomized controlled trial with 500 participants. Intervention includes
        weekly CHW sessions focusing on nutrition and physical activity.
        
        BUDGET JUSTIFICATION
        $150,000 for CHW training, $75,000 for participant incentives.
